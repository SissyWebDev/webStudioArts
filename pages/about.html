About Cesily Stevens and webStudioArts

Cesily began her digital journey in the early 1990s, attending technical college for graphic design. Coinciding with the release of Photoshop 1.0, she was fascinated by this burgeoning technology—so new to the field at the time that she learned the previous decade's manual layout techniques in tandem with this digital marvel. Working across many projects over the ensuing 20 years, she taught herself Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro, and Adobe Flash. In the mid-2000s, she began learning the principles and practices of modern web design. With endless possibilities for communicating her clients' needs—primarily other small business owners—she discovered the satisfaction of offering fully integrated digital design packages.

Today, Cesily continues to follow her passion for turning her clients' visions for outreach into beautiful, robust, accessible websites, utilizing her broad experience across modern digital marketing. Bringing a joyful curiosity to each aspect of the design/build process, Cesily excels in moving from imagination to tangible creations. Much of her satisfaction derives from her clients' reactions upon seeing their visions brought to life!
If a task does requires skills outside her preferred scope, she draws on relationships cultivated over the years with a wide range of digital creators. Her resourcefulness makes webStudioArts a meeting place where like-minded creators find avenues for diverse web-based solutions that add up to long-term satisfaction in the digital marketplace.

When not pecking away in a dimly lit room at her latest web creation, Cesily loves to be outdoors in the Pacific Northwest's temperate rainforest. The misty dales and rolling hills bring her endless joy and appreciation for the life's simplest pleasures -- strolling through verdant forests, foraging herbs or mushrooms, jumping in cold streams, and enjoying a warm cup with friends once safely back at home.
